# Province Preference Notes

The province matching logic is a ranking aid, not an official textbook assignment table.

Why:
- ChinaTextbook stores PDFs by stage, subject, publisher/version, and title, not by province.
- Some subjects are broadly unified, while many subjects still vary by province, city, district, school, and school year.
- Public web pages often discuss versions but rarely provide a complete current official province-by-province table.

Safe behavior:
- Prefer `统编版` for `语文`, `道德与法治`, and `历史`.
- Use province preferences as score boosts for other subjects.
- Let explicit user version terms such as `人教版`, `苏教版`, or `北师大版` override province preferences.
- Ask for confirmation when candidates differ by publisher, semester, or material type.

Starter preference examples:
- 安徽: `统编版`, `人教版`
- 江苏: `统编版`, `苏教版`, `译林版`, then `人教版`
- 北京: `统编版`, `北师大版`, then `人教版`
- 上海: `统编版`, `沪教版`, then `人教版`
- 广东/深圳: `统编版`; math often benefits from `北师大版` before `人教版`; English may prefer `沪教版`/`外研版`
- 浙江: `统编版`, `浙教版`, then `人教版`
- 山东: `统编版`; some subjects may prefer `鲁教版`/`鲁科版`, then `人教版`
- 河南/四川: `统编版`, `人教版`

Useful source types for updates:
- Ministry national textbook catalogs for统编 and nationally approved versions.
- Provincial/city/district education department textbook-selection notices.
- Official school textbook order lists for the target year.

When updating the table, prefer province education department or official school textbook announcement sources. Avoid treating SEO pages or forum claims as authoritative.
